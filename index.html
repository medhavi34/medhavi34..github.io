Research Experience

Transfer learning-based approach for detecting COVID-19 ailment in lung CT scan
The research aims to identify COVID-19 through various deep learning models on enhanced lung CT-SCAN images through a super-residual dense neural network. The experiment utilizes benchmark datasets like SARS-COV-2 CT-Scan and Covid-CT Scan. Pre-trained deep learning models such as XceptionNet, MobileNet, InceptionV3, DenseNet, ResNet50, and VGG16 have been employed. By including super resolution in the pre-processing step, the deep learning models show an increased accuracy, precision, recall and F1 score. The MobileNet model achieves a precision of 94.12% and 100% on the Covid-CT scan and SARS-COV-2 CT-Scan respectively.
